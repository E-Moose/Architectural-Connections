Competitions

Past and current competitions run by Architectural Connections for BCIT Students
Make sure to follow us on facebook or instagram to be notified of new contests.

Park[ing] Design Competition

The objective of this competition is to design a conceptual and creative urban or architectural intervention for two parking spaces on the BCIT campus. This competition is open to all BCIT students; designs may be submitted individually or in teams (five members maximum). The winning design will receive a $50 gift card! All designs will be shown at our End-of-Term Design Showcase & Mixer on Wednesday, December 6th @ 2:30pm in NE1 239!
Deadline: Tuesday, December 5th @ 11:59pm

Gingerbread House Design Contest

Design an architecturally-inspired gingerbread house at our 2nd Annual Gingerbread House Design Contest! "Building" materials will be provided and prizes awarded to the most creative designs! Spots are limited, so please sign your team (of four) up to reserve your spot!
Date: Wednesday, December 6th @ 2:30pm
Location: NE1 239

#captureBCIT Photography Competition

Photos must be posted by noon on Tuesday, December 5th.

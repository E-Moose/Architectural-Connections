Parking Design Competition

The objective of this competition is to design a conceptual and creative urban or architectural intervention for two parking spaces on the BCIT campus. This competition is open to all BCIT students; designs may be submitted individually or in teams (five members maximum). The designs will be reviewed anonymously by a panel of Architectural Connections Executives and ARSC and/or INTD guest judges. Prizes will be awarded to the top three designs, with the winning design receiving a $50 gift card.

3. Graphics and Icons

The Noun Project

A website that has many icons to use for a variety of reasons.

Go to


Freepik

Freepik offers users high quality vector graphic designs. Their content is filtered and free.

Go to


Map Stack

A website that has an interactive map (like google maps) but you can add layers and make interesting map styles.

Go to


Trianglify Generator

Drastically improve your colour palettes with this handy tool!

Go to


Adobe Colour Wheel

Easily find colour palettes following different rules.

Go to


Textures

A website that offers digital pictures of all sorts of materials. They have a large variety of materials and scenes at high resolutions and seamless edges.

Go to


SketchUp Texture

A good resource of textures and material images for use in sketch-up and other modelling and rendering software. Not the fanciest, but decent.

4. Cut-out People

Skalgubbar

A website to get PNG files of people to use with photoshop

Go to


ArchitechX

This website has PNG downloads of cutout figures and vegetation to populate your Renders and visualizations.

Go to


Architextur

Another useful website with PNG cutouts for your renders. Also has a Texture library.

Go to


Mr. Cutout

This Link is great for cut out people when doing renders for projects. There is a pretty diverse range of age and ethnicity.

Go to


ArchDaily

These are good if you would like a wider variety than just the Nordic people of Skalgubbar

Go to


Pimp my Drawing

These are less for photoshop drawings than they are for quickly adding scale people into AutoCAD sections, it's nice to get a sense of the scale of your building while designing in CAD or for printing out quick sections to draw over.
